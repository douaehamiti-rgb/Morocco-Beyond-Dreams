02 · Natural Wonders

Where earth whispers
in gold and blue.

Sahara Desert

Erg Chebbi's amber dunes rise 150m from the earth. Champagne under a billion stars, in linen tents lit by oil lamps. Dawn camel ride to the crest.

Ouzoud Waterfalls

Three tiers of turquoise cascading 110m into emerald pools. Barbary macaques watch from the cedar trees above. The air tastes of cool mist and wild mint.

Dakhla · The Atlantic Edge

Where the Sahara meets the Atlantic — kitesurfers carve glass-calm lagoons at the edge of the world. Legzira's red sandstone arches frame the setting sun.
